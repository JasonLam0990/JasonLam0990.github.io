题目重述

实现一个 HardMan:
HardMan(“jack”) 输出:
I am jack

HardMan(“jack”).rest(10).learn(“computer”) 输出
I am jack
//等待10秒
Start learning after 10 seconds
Learning computer

HardMan(“jack”).restFirst(5).learn(“chinese”) 输出
//等待5秒
Start learning after 5 seconds
I am jack
Learning chinese
